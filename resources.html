Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum investment for EB-5?

The minimum investment is $800,000 for projects in Targeted Employment Areas (TEA) or $1,050,000 for non-TEA projects.

How long does the EB-5 process take?

The entire process typically takes 24-36 months from initial filing to conditional green card approval, depending on your country of origin.

Can my family get green cards too?

Yes! Your spouse and unmarried children under 21 can be included as derivative beneficiaries in your EB-5 application.

What happens to my investment?

Your investment must remain at risk for the required period (typically 2 years). After condition removal, you may be eligible for return of capital.

Do I need to manage the business?

For Regional Center investments, you can be a passive investor. Direct investments require active management and involvement.

What if my petition is denied?

If denied, you may be able to appeal or refile. We work to minimize this risk through thorough preparation and documentation.
